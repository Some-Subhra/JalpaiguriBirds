A Li'l Bit Of Info About Us

Moderators

Biswapriya Rahut

A businessman by profession, his association with wildlife dates back to his childhood days when he used to walk the forests of Baikunthapur Division with their family estate malis. He took to birdwatching towards the late 90s after he was gifted with The Book of Indian Birds by Dr. Salim Ali on one of his birthdays. From the latter half of 2000s he had shifted his focus on the Hill Birds of Eastern Himalayas after he met  Chewang Bonpo,  another very gifted Birding Guide from South Sikkim. Now he concentrates his observation mainly on the birds of the Eastern Himalayas and Northeastern Hill States with a special emphasis on Wren Babblers. Quite a few of his articles have been published in National and International journals.


Mousumi Dutta

A Master Degree holder in Physics, she is currently employed with the India Meteorological Department as a Scientific Assistant. She has also successfully completed Basic and Advanced Mountaineering Course from Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Nehru Institute of Mountaineering. Ms. Dutta started birdwatching from the mid-2000s. She almost single handedly initiated "jalpaiguribirds.com" only the second of its kind in the state. Currently she is researching on the co-relation of arrival of Jacobian Cuckoo and the arrival of monsoon.


Contributors

Amit Thakurta

A school teacher by profession, he has been actively observing and photographing birds for the last 15 years. He has been instrumental in recording the occurrence of many new species in and around Jalpaiguri town. He is also a major contributor to the database of jalpaiguribirds.com. Many of his photographs and articles have been published in major Bird books and magazines. He also takes keen interest in painting.


Biplab Adhikary

A school teacher by profession, Mr. Adhikary is a passionate Wildlife photographer. He concentrates a major part of his work in the forests of North Bengal. He also takes keen interest in trekking and gardening. He is also closely associated with Jalpaiguri Photographic Association.


Supriyo Ghatak

A lecturer of Mechanical Engineering, Sri Ghatak is a keen birdwatcher and bird photographer. He is currently in-charge of the Falakata Polytechnic College. He has made some very significant records around the Jalpaiguri landscape and has quite a few major contribution for jalpaiguribirds.com.


Debapratim Saha

A Master Degree holder in Zoology, he is currently engaged in the teaching profession as a senior teacher. Mr. Saha is one of the most inevitable names in the elite panel of Bird Photographers of the current generation. Many of his works have appeared in both National and International magazines.


Amitava Basu

Amitava Basu has taken to Bird Photography only in the recent past, and within a very short time has established name in the birding fraternity. He is currently employed with the Panchayet Department of the state. Mr. Basu′s passion for birds takes him to all parts of North Bengal, Sikkim and Bhutan.


Hirak Sarkar

Currently employed with the Panchayet Department, Hirak Sarkar is a very passionate Wildlife Photographer. He has made some very important contributions in documenting many rare species in the forests of North Bengal. He has also played a very active role to support the cause of conservation in North Bengal.


Anupam Mistry

This young birdwatcher has almost single-handedly brought the Gormara NP-Chapramari WLS complex into National limelight through his immaculate observation and dedication. He has been instrumental in recording distribution of many rare and poorly understood species in the forests of northern West Bengal. An employee of the State Govt., Anupam spends most of his spare time and holidays in field observing wildlife.
